    Overview

    Throughout my academic journey, I've undertaken a variety of projects, ranging from captivating games and intriguing research endeavors to small, yet impactful programs. These projects not only showcase my coding prowess but also serve as a testament to my dedication and creativity in the realm of computer science. Feel free to explore them to get a glimpse of my coding style and the functionality each project brings to the table.

    In my approach to projects, I always consider the grand scheme, where every current action shapes the future. My favorite endeavors are those that have a positive impact or can be applied to benefit future endeavors. I find joy in sharing my ideas with others and value the incorporation of feedback into refining and enhancing my projects.

    As I embark on the next phase of my journey, I am actively seeking a full-time position that aligns with my passion for software engineering, data structures, AI algorithms, or other exciting realms within computer science. While I'm still exploring the diverse fields within this dynamic discipline, I am eager to contribute my skills and enthusiasm to a team that shares a vision for shaping the future of technology.

    If you have any questions, opportunities, or just want to connect, please don't hesitate to contact me. Let's explore the possibilities and work together to make a meaningful impact in the world of computer science.

    Here's to the next adventure!

    Background

    In addition to my academic pursuits, I am an individual who values a well-rounded and enriching lifestyle. I find joy in a variety of activities, such as hanging out with friends, rock climbing, hiking, gaming, programming, streaming, traveling, trying new foods, and visiting family. These diverse interests not only contribute to my personal growth but also provide a balanced and dynamic perspective to my life.

    At the age of 20, during the summer of 2022, I embarked on a transformative journey to Europe with two friends. This immersive experience, spanning over two weeks, was a culmination of years of hard work and dedication, both personally and academically. The trip not only served as a reward but also deepened my appreciation for travel and the richness of experiencing different cultures. I consider myself extremely fortunate to have had such an opportunity at a young age, recognizing that many may never venture beyond their own borders.

    In 2023, I achieved a significant personal milestone by completing a challenging hike to the summit of New York's highest mountain, Mount Marcy. This accomplishment not only fueled my passion for outdoor adventures but also underscored the importance of perseverance and determination.

    Looking ahead, I am eagerly anticipating an upcoming trip to Peru in March 2024. This journey promises to be another chapter of exploration and cultural immersion, further adding to my repertoire of global experiences.

    In my earlier years, I was actively involved in sports, playing soccer for numerous years until the age of 12, when my team disbanded. Transitioning to eighth grade, I took up swimming, a passion that accompanied me through high school. Achieving a remarkable milestone, I made sectionals all four years and was honored to serve as the swim team captain for three of those years. These experiences not only instilled discipline and teamwork but also showcased my commitment to excellence both in and out of the water.

    As I continue to explore new avenues and embrace diverse experiences, I remain open to the ever-evolving tapestry of life, ready to weave in new threads of knowledge and adventure.
